About Us

Lunch Ninja connects the NYU community. The vision behind Lunch Ninja is to give NYU students and staff the opportunity to be able to go on lunches with other NYU students with their choice of NYU School and Departments. The users even get to select their preferred type of cuisine, conversation interests and Voila, the algorithm will find the best Lunch mate for you!

Steps to lunch Ninja

  • Welcome to lunchNinja, since you're viewing this page you've already signed up with us. Great!
  • Select your preferences by clicking on the settings page which will help us match you with your perfect lunchmate.
  • Wait for the algorithm to do it's magic and get you matched.
  • Once you're matched, you'll be sent out details of your lunch mate as well as restaurant recommendations via email.
  • We'll send you feedback form after the lunch to help us improve lunchNinja.
  • Real time sequence of events can viewed on the Lunch History page.
